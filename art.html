Fine Art

Feels Monsters

These characters are intended to personify a single emotional state - I want the viewer to look at one of these characters, and immediately feel what that character is feeling.

All the Feels

Strong, complex, overwhelming feeling - I believe it's important to Feel Everything As Real, to explore the entirety of each experience. All of these feelings are fleeting, and worth exploring while they are present. They add context, and provide valuable insight into one's self and surroundings.

Sacred Birds

Sacred Geometry and timelessness, here to remind us of what's important in life. Time and space are vast, and we needn't get caught up in the small stuff. These sacred birds of the aeons remind us to keep it all in perspective.
